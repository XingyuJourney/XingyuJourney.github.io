Bennerley Viaduct

An old bridge in Ilkeston. Completed in 1877, it carries the Derbyshire branch of the Great Northern Railway and crosses the River Erewash and its broad, flat valley which forms the county boundary.

The windmill and surrounding farms can be seen from the Bennerley Viaduct
