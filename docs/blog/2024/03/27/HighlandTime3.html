The Journey of Xingyu ZHU

Ben Nevis

Conclusion: In harsh weather, it is unwise to climb the snowy mountain alone. If your equipment is inadequate and time is short, it is best to avoid the journey altogether.

Stage zero

At first I didn’t realize the seriousness of this situation.

Stage one

As I started to climb, the sky in front of me turned cloudy, but it looked like good weather behind me.


Stage two

At this time, I had climbed halfway up the mountain. Although visibility was poor, it was not snowing.


Stage three

When I climbed above the snow line and entered the final stage, I encountered a blizzard. Because I didn’t have basic equipment such as gloves and goggles, I felt like I was in prison from that moment on. The visibility was very low and I could only see the road under my feet.


Leaving

After the sun went down, I finally reached the foot of the mountain.
