## Nevis Range

### Located near Fort William, the Nevis Range is a well-known outdoor activity area where I stayed for two nights. The mountain resort offers bike rentals, and there are several scenic routes suitable for cycling (some are quite dangerous). I took the gondola up to the upper slopes — the designated ski area — but due to the season, there was no significant snowfall, so skiing wasn’t possible (a bit of a regret). When I first stepped off the gondola, the summit was shrouded in thick mist, creating an almost ethereal atmosphere.
